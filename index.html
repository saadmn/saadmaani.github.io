Why 20% of hospital patients do not respect their appointments?

The dataset collected information on 100,000 medical appointments in Brazil with 12 columns that indicated age, neighborhood, and SMS reception... after long hours of analysis since the data showed a normal distribution between the variables, I identified a good reason why this 20% of patients miss their appointments, which is the long waiting period (months) until the scheduled day, which could lead the patient to other alternatives so that he/she no longer needs the appointment.
